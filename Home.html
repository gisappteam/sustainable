About SustainMate

SustainMate is a next-generation AI chatbot designed to empower individuals and organizations to live and operate more sustainably. From reducing single-use plastics to optimizing supply chains for eco-efficiency, SustainMate is your all-in-one green lifestyle coach.

Features

  • 🌿 Personalized Tips: Get daily recommendations based on your habits and local data.
  • 📊 Eco Habit Tracking: Measure your footprint and celebrate your progress over time.
  • 🔁 Circular Economy Guide: Discover ways to reuse, recycle, and redesign your life.
  • 🤖 SDG Education: Learn interactively about the importance of sustainable development.

Why SDG 12?

Goal 12 — Responsible Consumption and Production — is key to a sustainable future. SustainMate helps you take action through AI-driven suggestions that reduce waste, improve energy use, and encourage mindful decisions. Whether you're shopping, cooking, or managing a business, your choices matter.
